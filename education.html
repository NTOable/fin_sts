Cybersecurity Awareness Simulation

You have experienced several simulated online fraud scenarios.

1. Suspicious Links

Attackers post malicious links in forums or messages to redirect users to harmful websites.

How to detect

  • Unknown users posting links
  • Promises of security tools or rewards
  • Shortened or strange URLs

How to avoid

  • Do not click links from strangers
  • Check the website domain
  • Use antivirus and browser protection

2. Ad Bomb Pop-ups

Ad-bombs overwhelm users with pop-ups to create panic and push them to install malicious software.

3. Fake Login Pages

Phishing websites copy real login pages to steal credentials.

4. Giveaway Scams

Scammers promise rewards to trick users into revealing personal data.

5. Urgent Account Warnings

Fake security alerts pressure users into making quick decisions.
